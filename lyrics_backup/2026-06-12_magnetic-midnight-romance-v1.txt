[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
When the moon pulls the tide, you pull my soul
I lose my breath when you take control
A quiet spark in the fading light
I want to lose myself in your eyes tonight

[Verse 1]
Shadows dance across this wooden floor
Every time you smile, I'm wanting more
We leave the noise of the world behind
You are the secret I'm so glad to find

[Pre-Chorus]
The hour grows late but the fire burns deep
Promises we silently swear to keep
I trace the lines of your soft, warm hand
No need for words, we understand

[Chorus]
It's a slow-burn draw, a magnetic pull
My heart is heavy and my cup is full
I lean in close to taste your sigh
We're painting our love on this midnight sky
